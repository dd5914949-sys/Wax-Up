Why hard wax?

Gentle on skin, tough on hair.

Unlike soft wax that adheres to both hair and epidermis (ouch!), our premium hard wax hardens and shrink-wraps each strand, allowing removal without stripping live skin cells. The result: dramatically less redness, minimal discomfort, and smoother results that last longer. We carry only phthalate‑free, cruelty‑free formulations.

  • Suitable for sensitive and Brazilian areas
  • Reduces risk of ingrown hairs
  • No muslin strips – healthier for the environment

Curious?

Questions we love to answer

Do you reuse wax applicators?

Absolutely not. We use a new, sterile wooden stick for each dip into the wax pot. Once it touches your skin, it’s discarded. This is our most sacred rule.

Is hard wax safe for all skin types?

Yes — it’s ideal for sensitive, reactive skin and delivers superior results on coarse hair. Our formulas are hypoallergenic and free of common irritants.

What if I have a skin condition (eczema/psoriasis)?

We always do a patch test and consultation first. In many cases, hard wax is well-tolerated, but we prioritize your health and may recommend alternative timing.

Do you double glove? Change bed covers?

Every guest receives a fresh, medical-grade linens or disposable bed cover. Our estheticians change gloves between every client and after any contact with non-sterile surfaces.
