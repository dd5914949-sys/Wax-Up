Hygiene: Our Non-Negotiable

In the heart of Chicago, we’ve created a sanctuary where your health comes first. Trust is earned through every applicator we use and every surface we sanitize.

The "No Double Dipping" Policy

We never reuse spatulas. A fresh, disposable applicator is used for every single dip into our wax pots. This prevents cross-contamination and ensures your skin is treated in a 100% sterile environment.

Hard Wax Only

For intimate and sensitive areas, we use only high-grade hard wax. Unlike strip wax, it shrink-wraps around the hair, not the skin, significantly reducing pain and redness.
